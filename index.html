STAY WELLBEING & LIFESTYLE RESORT

The resort is located on the Southern part of Phuket, situated within minutes from the hidden gems Rawai and Nai Harn beaches.

Decorated with modern perspective, and designed to cater the best possible comfort and experiences for peace seekers.

This all-in-one Phuket wellness resort offers fitness, weight loss and detox wellness retreats.

STAY Wellbeing & Lifestyle Resort features 162 private suites and villas with top-of-the-range facilities.

FITNESS, WEIGHT LOSS, DETOX RETREATS 

Look no further for your Thailand retreat, STAY Wellbeing & Lifestyle Resort offers different type of wellness retreats in Phuket, Thailand:

 Fitness retreat

 Weight loss retreat

Detox retreat (burnout retreat, stress retreat,…)

Our Phuket retreats offer a wide range of services, ranging from personal trainer, fitness group classes, food and beverage menus, bootcamp, dedicated wellness manager and much more…                  

Wellness retreat Thailand. Wellness retreat Phuket

STAY FIT FITNESS CENTER

1,000 sqm fitness center,  3 floors, and open 24hrs. Considered as one of the best gyms in Phuket, STAY Fit is comprised of three floors of top-of-the-line Life Fitness equipment.

Choose from group classes daily, ranging from Yoga at our dedicated studio & Circuit training training  (Station 10 HIIT, a program exclusive to STAY Wellbeing & Lifestyle Resort) , Eight Muay Thai, Spinning and Bootcamp. 

Group classes are taught by our professional trainers & accessible to all levels.

Our personal trainers are also available for one on one session to provide each individual a personal training plan according to their needs. Phuket gym.

LESPA BY STAY

This Phuket spa is considered as one of the best spa in Phuket.

This Phuket spa includes 2 Wet Onsen areas, 2 floating therapy tanks, ice bath, 17 treatment rooms for treatments, relax room and male, female lockers.

RESTAURANTS

At Fresca Kitchens & Deli, you will be treated to an electric, fun, yet casual bistro with an undeniable touch of class. Focused on sourcing quality products.

Unwind by one of our four swimming pools and savor your favorite beverages at STAY Green Cafe in Phuket. Our café is focused on healthy cuisine.

Whether you’re craving the bold flavors of Mexican or Thai cuisine or simply seeking a relaxing post-dinner drink, visit CHICA Rooftop Lounge to elevate your dining experience!
